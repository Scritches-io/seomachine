## Internal Linking Best Practices

1. **Link Naturally**: Only link when it helps the reader
2. **Vary Anchor Text**: Do not repeat the exact same phrase every time
3. **3 to 5 Links Per Post**: Ideal range for most posts
4. **Deep Link**: Prefer a feature or use-case page over the homepage
5. **Early Links Matter**: Place at least one internal link in the first 2 to 3 paragraphs
6. **Match Search Intent**: Use feature links for “how it works” and pricing links for “is it worth it”
7. **Avoid Clustering**: Space links out. Do not dump them all in one paragraph
