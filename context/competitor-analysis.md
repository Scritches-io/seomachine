## Competitive Landscape Overview

The pet care software market breaks into three segments:

1. **Pet Sitting & Dog Walking Software** (direct competitors): Time To Pet, Pet Sitter Plus, PetPocketbook, Precise Petcare, Scout
2. **Facility-Focused Software** (daycare/boarding/grooming): Gingr, ProPet, MoeGo
3. **Marketplaces** (consumer-facing): Rover, Wag

Scritches competes primarily in segment 1, targeting solo pet sitters and small teams who want modern, transparent booking software without enterprise complexity.

## Direct Software Competitors

### Time To Pet
- **Website**: timetopet.com
- **Target**: Pet sitting and dog walking businesses of all sizes
- **Pricing**: Lite $25/month, Solo $50/month, Team $40/month + $16/active staff, Large Team custom, Facility $80/month
- **Users**: 4,000+ businesses
- **Market Position**: Category leader in pet sitting software

**Key Features**: Scheduling, GPS tracking, client portal, invoicing, automated messaging, staff management, QuickBooks integration, mobile apps, report cards

**Content Strategy**:
- Extensive blog covering pet care business topics (scheduling, pricing, insurance, contracts, how-to-start guides)
- Rate calculators (pet sitting, dog walking) that rank well and drive traffic
- Owns top positions for core software keywords
- Publishes comparison and educational content regularly

**SEO Strengths**:
- 8,938 organic keywords with 1,934 in top 10
- Ranks #1 for "pet sitting software", "best pet sitting software", "pet care business software", "pet sitting contract"
- Rate calculator pages generate significant organic traffic
- Blog content covers nearly every pet care business topic

**SEO Weaknesses**:
- Content can feel templated and impersonal
- Broad approach means some topics lack depth
- Heavy reliance on informational keywords with lower conversion intent

**Scritches vs Time To Pet Differentiation**:
- Transparent booking (public pricing/availability vs hidden behind inquiry walls)
- No forced client account creation
- Modern payment support (Venmo, Zelle tracking)
- Modular forms/agreements not locked to rigid onboarding flow
- Deposit handling as first-class feature

### Pet Sitter Plus
- **Website**: petsitterplus.com
- **Target**: Solo sitters to teams of 25+ staff
- **Pricing**: Solo $30/month, Team $20/month + $8/active user, Large Team (25+) $270/month flat
- **Market Position**: Established mid-tier player, focused on feature depth

**Key Features**: Scheduling, client management, GPS tracking, invoicing, staff management, online booking, report cards, Xero/QuickBooks integration

**Content Strategy**:
- Minimal blog presence - very few published articles
- Relies more on organic branded searches and referral directories (Capterra, G2)
- Limited SEO investment visible in only 252 organic keywords

**SEO Strengths**:
- Ranks on page 1 for "pet sitting software" (#7) and "best pet sitting software" (#3)
- Homepage ranks well for branded and core software terms

**SEO Weaknesses**:
- Only 252 organic keywords and 697 monthly visits
- Almost no content marketing or blog strategy
- Weak top-of-funnel presence for informational queries
- No educational resources or calculator tools

**Scritches vs Pet Sitter Plus Differentiation**:
- Modern UI vs dated interface
- Public booking pages vs inquiry-first flow
- Active content marketing strategy
- Scritches already has a comparison post ranking #12 for "pet sitter plus alternatives"

### PetPocketbook
- **Website**: petpocketbook.com
- **Target**: Solo pet sitters and small operations
- **Pricing**: Free tier available, paid plans from ~$25/month
- **Market Position**: Budget-friendly entry point, specifically branded as "software for the modern dog walker"

**Key Features**: Invoicing, scheduling, client management, automated reminders, GPS tracking, report cards, online booking, commission calculations, medication reminders, pet diary, QuickBooks integration

**Content Strategy**:
- Moderate blog presence with some SEO-targeted content
- 982 organic keywords suggest some content investment
- Focus on practical how-to content for pet sitters

**SEO Strengths**:
- Ranks for "pet sitting software" (#10) and "dog walking software" (#4)
- Reasonable keyword diversity at 982 keywords
- Free plan drives organic mentions and reviews

**SEO Weaknesses**:
- Only 47 top 10 rankings despite having ~1K keywords
- Most keywords rank in positions 11-100
- Blog content quantity and quality below Time To Pet

**Scritches vs PetPocketbook Differentiation**:
- More comprehensive feature set (forms, agreements, surcharges, review booster)
- Public booking landing pages with full transparency
- Better positioning for businesses that want professional-grade tools, not just basic invoicing

**Scritches vs Precise Petcare Differentiation**:

### Scout for Pets
- **Website**: scoutforpets.com
- **Target**: Pet care businesses from solo to 100+ employees
- **Pricing**: Solo $33/month ($39/month annual), Team $33/month + $15/active staff ($39 + $18 annual). Add-ons: Branded App $300/month, SEO Website $42/month, Paid Ads $1,000/month
- **Users**: 1,100+ companies
- **Market Position**: Growing mid-market player with strong content strategy

**Key Features**: Client scheduling, GPS tracking, photo check-ins, automated billing, staff management, electronic service agreements, payroll integration, reporting, branded client app (white-label), mobile apps

**Content Strategy**:
- Active blog with SEO-targeted articles
- 3,144 organic keywords (second-highest among sitter/walker software after Time To Pet)
- Publishes comparison pages ("vs Time To Pet", "vs competitors")
- Content about rates, starting businesses, and industry topics

**SEO Strengths**:
- 16,881 monthly organic visits - growing fast
- Ranks #4 for "time to pet alternatives" with dedicated comparison page
- Appears for rate-related queries ("pet sitting rates" #12, "overnight pet sitting rates" #8)
- 682 top 10 rankings with 118 in positions 2-3

**SEO Weaknesses**:
- Still significantly behind Time To Pet in total keyword coverage
- Blog content depth sometimes lacks the detail needed for top positions
- Many keywords still in positions 11-20 (617 keywords) - striking distance

**Scritches vs Scout Differentiation**:
- Transparent, public booking pages (pricing/availability visible without inquiry)
- No forced account creation for clients
- Modular forms and agreements workflow
- Modern payment flexibility (Venmo, Zelle, Cash App tracking)
- Scout's white-label client app is a feature Scritches doesn't currently offer

## Facility-Focused Software (Adjacent Competitors)

### Gingr
- **Website**: gingrapp.com
- **Target**: Boarding kennels, dog daycare, grooming salons, training facilities
- **Pricing**: Spa $105/month, Play $145/month, Stay $155-180/month, Enterprise custom
- **Users**: 10M+ pets processed, 150M+ reservations
- **Founded**: 2014

**Key Features**: Self-service online booking, PreCheck intake, integrated payments, business analytics, pet parent app, report cards, membership tools, multi-location management

**SEO Profile**: 15,080 keywords, 69,769 monthly visits. Massive content operation but targeting facility-related keywords (grooming, daycare, boarding). Not a direct SERP competitor for pet sitter/walker keywords.

**Relevance to Scritches**: Gingr doesn't compete for the same keywords or market. However, their SEO scale shows what's possible in pet care software content marketing. Some of their sitters could be prospects for Scritches if they offer in-home services.

### ProPet Software
- **Website**: propetsoftware.com (also propetware.com)
- **Target**: Boarding kennels, catteries, pet resorts, grooming salons, daycare
- **Pricing**: Base $49.99/month + modules (Boarding $20/month, Daycare $10/month, Training $10/month, Grooming $10/month). Setup fee required.

**SEO Profile**: No data available in DataForSEO. Minimal organic presence. Relies on directory listings (Capterra, G2, GetApp).

**Relevance to Scritches**: Not a SERP competitor. Different market entirely.

### MoeGo
- **Website**: moego.pet
- **Target**: Mobile groomers, grooming salons, boarding, daycare, training, pet sitting
- **Pricing**: Basic $49/month, Growth $99/month/van, Ultimate $159/month/van, Enterprise custom
- **Users**: 10,000+ businesses

**Key Features**: Smart scheduler, route optimization (for mobile groomers), online booking, automated reminders, review automation, digital agreements, marketing tools

**SEO Profile**: Appeared in SERP results for "pet sitting software" (#14) and "best pet sitting software" (#14). Primarily targets grooming keywords but expanding into the broader pet care space.

**Relevance to Scritches**: MoeGo is expanding into pet sitting territory, making them a potential future competitor. Worth monitoring as they have significant VC backing and a polished product. Their pricing page showed mobile grooming-focused tiers, suggesting pet sitting isn't their primary focus yet.

## Strategic Takeaways

### 1. Time To Pet is the content leader to beat
They own the most keywords, have the highest traffic, and rank #1 for core software terms. Competing head-on requires a sustained content investment over 6-12+ months. Focus on differentiation: Scritches' transparent booking, modern payments, and no-login-wall approach.

### 2. Scout is the fastest-growing content competitor
With 3,144 keywords and 16,881 monthly visits, Scout has invested heavily in content and comparison pages. Their "vs Time To Pet" page ranks #4 for alternatives queries. Scritches should study and replicate this comparison page strategy.

### 3. Biggest quick wins are striking-distance keywords
- "dog walking software" at #20 (needs 10 positions to reach page 1)
- "pet sitter plus alternatives" at #12 (needs 2 positions for page 1)
- Existing blog posts on rates, contracts, and forms need optimization to enter top 20

### 4. Calculator tools are high-value SEO assets
Time To Pet's rate calculators drive significant traffic. Scritches already has Rover/Wag fee calculators. Consider building a pet sitting rate calculator and dog walking rate calculator to compete.

### 5. Comparison content converts
"[Competitor] alternatives", "[Competitor] pricing", and "[Competitor] vs [Competitor]" keywords have high purchase intent. Scritches already has Time To Pet pricing and Pet Sitter Plus pricing posts. Add comparison pages for Scout, Precise Petcare, and PetPocketbook.

### 6. Informational content builds authority but needs volume
Keywords like "pet sitting rates" (3,600/mo), "dog walking rates" (3,600/mo), and "pet sitting insurance" (2,400/mo) drive large traffic volumes. Scritches has posts on some of these but they're not ranking yet. Consistent publishing and optimization will build the domain authority needed to compete.

### 7. Pet Sitter Plus and PetPocketbook are weak on content
Both have minimal blog strategies. Scritches can outflank them on SEO relatively quickly since they aren't investing in content. Focus comparison posts on these competitors to capture their "alternatives" traffic.

### 8. MoeGo is an emerging threat
MoeGo is well-funded, has a polished product, and is starting to rank for pet sitting keywords despite being grooming-focused. Monitor their expansion into the pet sitting space.
